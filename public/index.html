What is TRU Colors?

In 2017, 11 gang leaders came together to form TRU Colors Brewing and put a stop to the ongoing gang violence. Made up of active Bloods, Crips, and Growth & Development (formerly Gangster Disciples), we fight day in and day out to change the perception of gang culture and create a safer world.
