The Initiation

IPA

Style IPA
Flavor Orange Peel, Dried Japones Chiles, and Grapefruit Puree
ABV 7%
IBU 75
Availability All Year Round

The Story

The Initiation was inspired by the 6-week startup bootcamp that the co-founders of TRU Colors endured to form the company. Part of their initiation was to jump 14,000 ft. from an airplane. This beer is a representation that the change is bitter, the tension is hot, but the success is sweet.

The Neighborhood Hero

Strong Ale

Style Strong Ale
Flavor Strawberry, Blueberry, Blackberry
ABV 10%
IBU 50
Availability All Year Round

The Story

Once infamous villains and former nemesis, we’ve bound together for good. Brewers by day and soldiers by night, we take to the streets armed with passion, education, and influence fighting to put an end to violence in our city. The neighborhood hero is not just a great beer, but a commitment from all TCB members to be the change that we want to see in the world.

The Conversation

Vanilla Coffee Blonde

Style Blonde
Flavor Vanilla Coffee
ABV 5%
IBU 15
Availability All Year Round

The Story

This beer will be a representation of bridging the gap between our inner-city and outlying community through education and meaningful dialogue with a perfect blend of Vanilla and Coffee that sparks The Conversation.

The Brewery

Our brewery and brewpub won’t open until the first half of 2018. It’s a big project and takes some time to get going! In the meantime, we want to begin recruiting and vetting the rest of our team. We also want to provide opportunity to other gang members who want to work. To make both these things happen, we’ve setup the TCB Apprentice Program. Here’s the deal: Anyone who wants a position in the brewery must first successfully complete 2 months in the TCB Apprentice Program.
